#### Features
- **Adorable blob pet** with expressive eyes and smooth animations
- **AI behavior system** with multiple states (Idle, Walking, Sleeping, Eating, Playing)
- **Eye tracking** - pet's eyes follow your mouse cursor
- **Needs system** - Happiness, Energy, and Hunger stats that decay over time
- **Interactions** - Feed, Play, Pet, and Sleep commands
- **6 color options** - Blue, Pink, Green, Purple, Orange, Mint
- **Particle effects** - Hearts, Sparkles, ZZZ, and Food animations
- **Persistence** - Saves pet state, position, and statistics
- **Time-aware** - Calculates decay when you're away
- **Statistics tracking** - Times fed, played, and petted

#### Technical
- Built on .NET 8.0 with WPF
- Uses CommunityToolkit.MVVM 8.2.2
- Serilog 3.1.1 for logging
- Follows 3SC widget architecture patterns
- Smooth 60 FPS animations
- Multi-monitor support
